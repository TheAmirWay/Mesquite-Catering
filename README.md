Mesquite Catering CRM
A B2B corporate outreach platform built for Mesquite, a Phoenix based restaurant group, to support catering sales expansion.
Purpose:
Mesquite identified catering as a major untapped revenue channel. This tool was built to organize and manage outreach to large corporate accounts including pharmaceutical companies, healthcare organizations, and enterprise businesses in the Phoenix area.
Features:
273+ pre loaded corporate contacts across multiple industries
Contact details organized by company, industry, and outreach priority
Built for easy use by non technical staff to support sales outreach
Impact:
Created a structured pipeline from scratch to support Mesquite's catering growth strategy, giving the team a centralized starting point for B2B outreach instead of managing contacts manually.
