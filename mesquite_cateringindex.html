🔗 Apollo.io Integration Guide

What Apollo does for Mesquite Catering

This CRM has verified business phone numbers and addresses. Apollo.io finds the actual named person at each business — the GM at a dealership, the HR Director at a hospital — with their direct email and sometimes direct dial. That's the difference between calling a main line and asking for someone vs. emailing a specific person by name.

Recommended: Basic Plan — $49/mo annual or $59/mo monthly

1
Go to apollo.io and create a free account. Sign up for Basic plan ($59/mo monthly, no commitment). You get 1,000 email credits and 75 mobile credits per month — enough to cover this entire list.
2
In Apollo, go to People Search. Use these filters:
• Job Title: "Office Manager" OR "General Manager" OR "HR Director" OR "Events Coordinator"
• Company Name: Search each business from this CRM
• Location: Phoenix, AZ metro / Maricopa County
3
For dealerships: search dealership name + "Office Manager" or "General Manager"
For hospitals: search hospital name + "HR Director" or "Human Resources"
For corporate: search company + "Office Manager" or "Facilities Manager"
For hotels: search hotel name + "Events Coordinator" or "Catering Manager"
4
Export from Apollo as CSV. Then click + Add Contact in this CRM and paste in the named person's info. Or click the ✏ edit button on existing contacts to add the person's name and direct email from Apollo on top of the main phone number already here.
5
Best Apollo searches for this list:

• "Office Manager" + "Earnhardt Toyota" + Arizona
• "HR Director" + "Banner Health" + Mesa AZ
• "Events Coordinator" + "Fairmont Scottsdale" + Arizona
• "HR Director" + "Mesa Public Schools" + Arizona
• "General Manager" + "AutoNation" + Tempe AZ

Pro tip: Install Apollo's free Chrome extension. Then go to each dealership or hospital's LinkedIn page and Apollo shows you everyone who works there with their contact info — no searching needed.

ROI check

$59/month for Apollo. One dealership locked in for weekly Friday lunch (25 people × $14/person = $350/week = $1,400/month recurring). Apollo pays for itself in the first 2 hours of that first account. Do one month, export all contacts, cancel if you want — or keep it to keep finding new leads.
